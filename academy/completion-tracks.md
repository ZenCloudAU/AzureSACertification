# Completion Tracks

The course uses three tracks. Learners self-select per module based on their current depth and learning goal.

---

## Track 1 — Foundation

**Who:** Learners rebuilding recognition and conceptual fluency. Enough to participate in design reviews and ask informed questions.

**Commitment per module:** 45–90 minutes reading and review questions.

**Completion criteria:**

- Read the module (architect briefing + engineering view)
- Complete all review questions — written answers, not just recall
- Able to name the key Azure services in this module and state one failure mode for each
- Does not require lab completion or artefact production

**Sign-off:** Self-assessed. No formal sign-off required.

---

## Track 2 — Practitioner

**Who:** Learners rebuilding hands-on engineering fluency. Enough to contribute to design sessions, produce governance artefacts, and supervise engineering work.

**Commitment per module:** 2–4 hours including lab.

**Completion criteria:**

- Complete all Foundation criteria
- Complete the lab: all steps, all evidence captured
- Produce the artefact: filled template, not blank
- Answer the lab review questions — written answers
- Artefact reflects actual lab configuration, not generic text

**Sign-off:** Peer review or self-review against the lab completion checklist.

---

## Track 3 — Senior Architect

**Who:** Learners rebuilding the depth to lead design authority, govern cloud delivery, and produce engineer-ready design artefacts that can go directly into build.

**Commitment per module:** 4–6 hours including lab, senior review, and artefact annotation.

**Completion criteria:**

- Complete all Practitioner criteria
- Complete the senior SA review section: written answers, not summary notes
- Annotate the artefact with governance notes, trade-off decisions, and any risks accepted
- Identify at least one failure mode from this module that would be missed by a junior engineer
- Able to brief a team on this topic in 10 minutes without notes

**Sign-off:** Senior SA review sign-off required. See [senior-sa-review-rubric.md](senior-sa-review-rubric.md).

---

## Track Progression

Tracks are not sequential requirements — learners can complete any module at any track. However:

- Later modules build on earlier ones. Module 02 IAM assumes Module 01 platform control is understood.
- Artefact quality compounds. A practitioner who produces a weak RBAC matrix will struggle with the security module.
- Senior architect track in module 09 (Architecture-to-Engineering Translation) requires practitioner completion of modules 01–08.
