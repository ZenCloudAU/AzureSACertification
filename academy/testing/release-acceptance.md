## Release Process Notes

A phase is released when:
1. All content files in scope have status: Complete
2. QA checklist passes — QA Lead signs off
3. UX test checklist passes — UX Lead signs off
4. Azure technical validation passes — Senior SA signs off
5. PM confirms scope and sequencing
6. Product Owner gives final approval
7. This release-acceptance record is updated and committed to the repository

Content is not published to Velocity Academy until this record reflects full sign-off.
